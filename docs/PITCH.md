# UserGrow — Elevator Pitch

<!-- CI enforces: max 200 words. Current: ~175 words -->

**AI is the new search engine — but brands are flying blind.**

ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity influence millions of decisions daily, yet no brand knows what these models say about them, or *to whom*.

**UserGrow makes AI brand visibility measurable — and improvable — for the first time.** We continuously monitor AI models using census-backed demographic personas across languages, comparing recommendations against real-world ratings. SEO monitoring, but for the AI era.

What sets us apart technically: **Engram**, our neuroscience-grounded memory engine (ACT-R activation, Hebbian learning, Ebbinghaus forgetting), gives the agent real memory — it recalls what changed since last scan, spots drift patterns, and gets smarter with every analysis. **Causal inference** (Pearl's do-calculus) then answers *why* a brand's visibility shifted: was it a content change, a model update, or a competitor move?

Every week, your dashboard updates with share-of-voice trends, demographic blind spots, and reality mismatches — like being rated 4.9 stars but ranked #8 by ChatGPT. Over time, our longitudinal dataset becomes the moat: no one else tracks how AI recommendations shift week over week.

GEO is an early field. We identify the problems clearly and we're building the first optimization playbook as we go. **Launching private beta Q2 2025** — early partners get continuous monitoring and help shape the playbook.
